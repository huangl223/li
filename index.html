Hi, I am a lecturer in Computer Science at CUB . Prior to that, in 2025, I finished my PhD at Constructor Institute of Technology (Schaffhausen, Switzerland) and CUB, under the supervision of Prof. Bertrand Meyer . I finished my master and bachelor study in software engineering at Sun Yat-sen University (Guangzhou, China) in 2017 and 2019.

My research interests center around formal verification, in particular statistical model checking, program verification and interplay between AI and formal verification.

At Constructor University, my current responsibilities span lecturing and developing research that bridges software verification and learning-based techniques. I see all of these educational and research activities as different forms of the same mission: helping shape a generation of engineers who build software with both clarity and responsibility.
